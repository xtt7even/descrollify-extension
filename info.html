Descrollify — Help

Descrollify helps you spend less time on short videos. Here's how the modes work, what each option does, and answers to common questions.

The three modes

Descrollify has three modes. Pick the one that matches how much you want to cut back:

  • OFF — Nothing is blocked. Watch short videos as usual.
  • Limit — Set a daily number of short videos. Watch up to that many, then Descrollify blocks the rest.
  • Focus — Blocks all short videos and stops short-video links from opening, so nothing slips through. You can also hide their thumbnails so you don't even see them.

How to switch modes

  1. Click the Descrollify icon in your browser's toolbar.
  2. Choose a mode: OFF, Limit, or Focus.
  3. Change any extra settings if you want to.
  4. That's it — the mode is active right away.

Options Manual

1. Reset Statistics

Clears all the usage numbers Descrollify has tracked and starts your stats over from zero. Click RESET STATISTICS to do it.

2. Focus Mode options

Hide short video thumbnails

Hides the preview images for short videos so they don't tempt you. Turn it on by clicking "Hide short videos thumbnails" and making sure the radio button next to it is selected.

3. Limit Mode options

Max videos allowed

How many short videos you can watch before Descrollify starts blocking. Type a number from 1 to 30, or use the up/down arrows.

Remove blocker after

How long the block lasts once it kicks in. Set it with the Hours, Minutes, and Seconds dropdowns. Leave all three at 0 if you want the block to stay until you change the mode yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it block short videos everywhere?
For now it only blocks YouTube Shorts. Support for other platforms is in the works.
Does it track my history or collect my data?
No. Descrollify never looks at your watch or browser history. It only counts the short videos you scroll past to build your stats, and everything stays on your own computer. It doesn't save video links or any other personal information.
